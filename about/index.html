Sarah Holland

Sarah Holland is a film student at the University of Central Florida. She is an award-winning playwright and filmmaker, and has also acted in several student-written plays at UCF. Her one-act play Mind Games was produced at UCF in Fall 2015, and her one-act play Parasite was produced at UCF in Spring 2017. Her dark comedy Gagged was selected for screening at Campus MovieFest alongside 15 other films, and won Best Set Design. Her 3-minute short film In Bloom was selected for the FilmFreeway festival in Summer 2017. Sarah's alternative beauty channel DyeYoung has over 40,000 subscribers and her second channel shollandTV maintains over 15,000 subscribers.
